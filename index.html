FAQ

Questions leaders usually ask before starting.

Is this training or consulting?

The scorecard is a diagnostic front-end. Depending on the results, the next step may be AI enablement training, workflow design, or done-for-you automation consulting.

Do we need technical teams involved?

Not always at the beginning. The scorecard is designed to help operators and business leaders clarify workflow readiness before over-involving engineering or vendors.

Can this work for non-technical teams?

Yes. The focus is practical adoption: workflows, human review, automation fit, and team behavior — not abstract AI theory.

What tools do you work with?

The brand strategy highlights applied generative AI, n8n automation, APIs, no-code systems, and agentic workflows. Tool choice should follow workflow diagnosis, not the other way around.
